💡 Tips for Accurate Readings

  • 🕒 Always read the meter at the same time of day when possible
  • 🔍 Make sure to read all digits including decimal places
  • 📷 Take a photo of your meter for reference
  • 📊 Regular readings help track your consumption patterns
  • ⚠️ For prepaid meters, readings decrease as you use electricity
